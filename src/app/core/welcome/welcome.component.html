IST 440W - Team 3 - 2020 Fall Semester

The purpose of this project is to design and implement a solution for the decryption of handwritten messages. The messages in question may consist of a wide range of languages, or possibly a language that is made-up. The minimum requirements our stakeholders have agreed to is three languages. Our stakeholders have agreed that a minimum of three encryption / decryption types need to be supported. With the complexity of this project, a multitude of functionalities will need to be integrated in order to have a seamless service that can be leveraged to solve the needs of our stakeholders.
